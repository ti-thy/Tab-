About

I study the physics of star formation in molecular clouds using multi-wavelength observations (JWST, ALMA, HST) and numerical simulations. My research focuses on protostellar feedback, turbulence in giant molecular clouds, and the initial mass function.

Education

  • Ph.D. Astrophysics – University of Cambridge, 2023
  • M.Sc. Astronomy – Leiden University, 2019
  • B.Sc. Physics – Caltech, 2017

Research Interests

Protostellar Outflows & Feedback

Tracing jets and winds with JWST NIRSpec IFU data.

Molecular Cloud Turbulence

Statistical analysis of velocity fields in nearby star-forming regions.

Initial Mass Function

Connecting core mass functions to stellar IMF across environments.
